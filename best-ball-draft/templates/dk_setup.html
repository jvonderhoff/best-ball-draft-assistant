🔗 BBA Live Setup

Run once on your DK draft page — BBA streams picks to your phone automatically.

Checking…
1
Go to your DK snake draft page

Open Chrome or Firefox on your Mac/PC. Navigate to your live draft:
draftkings.com/draft/snake/YOUR_DRAFT_ID

2
Open the browser console

Press F12 (Windows) or ⌘⌥I (Mac) → click the Console tab.

💡 If Chrome says "paste is not allowed", type allow pasting first and press Enter.
3
Copy and paste this snippet

Paste into the console and press Enter. You'll see log lines confirming picks were sent.

4
Open BBA on your phone

On your phone go to 192.168.1.161:8000/recommend, enter your draft ID, tap Connect. Picks will appear and update as the draft progresses.

💡 Leave the desktop tab open — the snippet re-runs every 15 seconds automatically.

Trust the certificate (one time)

Because this server uses a self-signed HTTPS cert, your browser needs to trust it once before the bookmarklet can reach it.

Open in Chrome on your phone. Tap Advanced → Proceed (or "Visit this website"). You'll see the normal app — that's it, done once ever.

1
Copy the "BBA Live" bookmarklet
2
Save it as a bookmark

Bookmark any page in Chrome (tap the ⋮ menu → Star). Then open your bookmarks, long-press the new bookmark, tap Edit bookmark. Delete the URL field, paste the snippet, save.

💡 Name it BBA Live so it's easy to find in the address bar.
3
Navigate to your DK draft room & tap BBA Live

Go to draftkings.com/draft/nfl/YOUR_DRAFT_ID in Chrome (your actual live draft page). Tap the address bar, type BBA, and select the BBA Live bookmark.

A green banner will appear: "✓ BBA Live! Intercepting picks on draft #…"

A small 🟢 BBA badge appears at the bottom-right — this means BBA is actively forwarding picks to your server.

4
Open BBA in another tab and enter your draft ID

Switch to a new tab, go to /recommend, enter your draft ID, and tap Connect. Picks will appear as they come in.

💡 Keep the DK tab open — BBA intercepts picks as DK's own app fetches them, so the DK tab must stay alive.
